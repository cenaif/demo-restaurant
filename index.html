Our Story

Recipes from the coast, a table in the city.

Kori & Neer was born from a craving — the kind only your grandmother's kori rotti on a rainy Mangalore afternoon can fix. We brought that feeling to Bengaluru.

Every dish is made from scratch. Our coconut is freshly grated each morning. Our spice blends are stone-ground in-house. Our neer dosa batter ferments overnight — the way it always should.

No fusion experiments. No shortcuts. Just honest coastal food, served warm, the way it was meant to be eaten.
